About Me

I was born and raised in Salt Lake City and decided I wanted to be a musician at a young age. Throughout my musical career I began to realize that no matter how good you are there are little to no career options for a drummer.
After spending some time at USU majoring in CS, I looked for an industry that I would enjoy, is challenging, and would keep me busy. With some basic c++ experience under my belt, I dove head first into the wonderful world of web development.
I am an extremely persistent learner and although I may not have all of the skills you want immediately, you'll be hard pressed to find someone else with my
work ethic and desire to learn some new skills.
I would love to be a part of your company, hope to hear from you soon!
